Lisa Harnum Wikipedia : Everything To Know About The Fianc Of Simon Gittany

Lisa Harnum, not available on Wikipedia, was a 30 years old woman whose fiancee killed on July 30, 2011.

She was killed tragically by her then-fiancé Simon Gittany. He threw her from the fifteenth-floor balcony of their apartment in the City of Sydney.

Her fiancee isolated her in the luxury apartment on the 15th floor of the exclusive Sydney CBD block. She had just discovered Gittany’s secret.

After regulating or cutting off her physical contact with almost any other human being, Simon was now monitoring her every electronic movement before killing her.

Spotlight On Lisa Harnum’s Wikipedia

The official Wikipedia does not feature the biography of Lisa.

Lisa was born in June 1981. Her mother raised her in Canada.

Growing up, She was interested in football and karate but loved music and dance even more. Her family members remembered her as someone who always had a smile on her face.

She was a talented dancer who studied tap, jazz, and ballet. She had even taken part in various competitions in Canada and the United States. Her mother had stated that she was diagnosed with an eating disorder early on, something Lisa had to work through all her life.

Learn About Lisa Harnum’s Family

There are not many details on Lisa’s family.

Her mother’s name is Joan.

Her family conducts the foundation in Lisa’s name to help the victims of domestic violence. They are rescuing and assisting many victims through the foundation.

Who Is Lisa Harnum’s Husband?

Lisa was not married but was engaged to Simon Gittany before her death.

They were engaged for six weeks.

He isolated her, and she was in the trap of violence by him.

Simon is in Parklea prison in charge of murdering Lisa. He was found guilty in November 2013 of throwing Ms. Harnum off the balcony of their Sydney city apartment in July 2011.

In February 2014, Simon was sentenced to a minimum of 18 years to 26 years in prison.
