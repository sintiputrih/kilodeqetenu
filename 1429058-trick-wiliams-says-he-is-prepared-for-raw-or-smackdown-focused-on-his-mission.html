Trick Wiliams Says He Is Prepared For RAW or SmackDown, Focused On His Mission

Could fans hear “Whoop That Trick” on WWE RAW or WWE SmackDown soon?

During an appearance on Busted Open Radio , Bully Ray asked NXT star Trick Williams whether he is prepared for RAW or SmackDown, and the NXT star responded by saying, “Absolutely.”

Williams was then asked whether he would want to move up to the main roster or continue in NXT until the right time. He stated that he is focused on the mission that is ahead of him, whatever that may be.

“To be honest, I’m a big proponent of being where your feet are. Whatever the mission is ahead of me, I want to kill that. If that means it’s RAW, it’s RAW. If it’s NXT, it’s NXT. Whatever the mission is, that’s where my feet are. I don’t try to look too far ahead most of the time. (H/t Jeremy Lambert of Fightful for the transcription)

Trick Williams won the Iron Survivor Challenge at NXT Deadline. As a result, he is set to challenge Ilja Dragunov for the NXT Champion at NXT New Year’s Evil on January 2. WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs.
