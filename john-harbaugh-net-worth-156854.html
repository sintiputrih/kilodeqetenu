John Harbaugh Net Worth

John Harbaugh is an NFL head coach who was born on September 23, 1962 in Toledo, OH. He has had a successful career as a defensive backs coach for over a decade before becoming the head coach of the Baltimore Ravens in 2008. He led the Ravens to victory in Super Bowl XLVII, cementing his legacy as one of the most successful coaches in the NFL.
