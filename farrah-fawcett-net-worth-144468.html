Farrah Fawcett Net Worth

Farrah Fawcett was an iconic sex symbol and pop star who rose to fame as Jill Munroe on Charlie's Angels. Born on February 2, 1947 in Corpus Christi, TX, she was voted TV Guide's 26th Greatest TV Star of All Time. Her fame was further solidified when a poster of her in a red bathing suit sold over twenty million copies, catapulting her to sex-symbol stardom.
