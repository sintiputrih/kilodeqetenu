Harry Styles returning to Scotland as 'Love on Tour' adds new 2023 dates

Harry Styles fans in Scotland will be thrilled to hear the musician has added a stop in Edinburgh in May 2023.

The 28-year-old announced extra dates for 'Love on Tour' with an additional 19 shows in the UK and Europe during the 2023 leg of the tour. Tickets for the Murrayfield gig on May 26, 2023 will go on sale September 2 at 10am.

It follows the former One Directioner's show in Glasgow that saw over 50,000 people flock to Ibrox for the gig - the stadium's first in almost 20 years - as superfans camped outside the venue in the days leading up to the concert this past June.
