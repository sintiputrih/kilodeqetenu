Amy Robach Salary, Age, House, Height, Instagram

Amy Joanne Robach is an American television reporter for ABC News.

She is the co-anchor of 20/20 and the breaking news anchor/fill-in anchor for Good Morning America.

Robach was a national correspondent for NBC News, co-host of the Saturday edition of NBC’s Today, and anchor on MSNBC.

Since May 2018, she has been the co-anchor of 20/20 alongside David Muir.

Amy Robach has been married to former Melrose Place star Andrew Shue since February 6, 2010.
