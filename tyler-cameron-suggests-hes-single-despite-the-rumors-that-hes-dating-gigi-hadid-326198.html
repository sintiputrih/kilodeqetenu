Tyler Cameron Suggests Hes Single Despite The Rumors That Hes Dating Gigi Hadid

Amid rumors that Tyler Cameron is dating Gigi Hadid, it sounds like the former Bachelorette contestant is actually single! While serving as a Watch What Happens Live bartender on the latest episode, he was asked by a guest, Jenny McCarthy, if he had found love or not.

In response, Tyler teased the fellow celeb by saying that ‘I may be single.’

This is actually not the first time he says something similar despite the many speculations that he and the supermodel are romantically involved.

Previously, while on E!’s Daily Pop, he stressed that he and Gigi are just platonic!

‘That is my friend. We hung out a few times. She is doing her thing now in Paris and killin’ it so…,’ Tyler told the co-hosts.

As fans and shippers of this couple remember, Tyler and Gigi were romantically linked for the first time over the summer and started to spend more and more time together, only adding to the speculations.

They were spotted on a number of so-called dates but usually accompanied by mutual friends as well.

The two even went to New York Fashion Week events together but chose to walk the red carpet separately.

Still, while at her grandmother’s funeral, he was not only there to support her during the hard time but they were even holding hands!
