Lucy Liu aka Lucy Alexis Liu is an American actress and model. As of 2023, Lucy Liu’s net worth is $16 million. Lucy had breakthrough performances with her role in Charlie’s Angels and Kill Bill Vol 1. She is the recipient of many honorary accolades including a Screen Actors Guild Award.

Lucy made her cinematic debut through her performance as Donna in the 1992 film, Rhythm of Dance. She then appeared in a handful of successful films including The Mating Habits of the Earthbound Human, City of Industry, Jerry Maguire, Bang, Payback, Molly, True Crime, etc.
