Bill Farmer Net Worth

Bill Farmer is an American voice actor best known for his work as the voice of Disney's Goofy, Pluto, and Horace Horsecollar. He was born on November 14, 1952 in Pratt, KS and took over the parts in the late 1980s. He has since become a beloved figure in the Disney universe, providing the voices for many of the company's most iconic characters.
