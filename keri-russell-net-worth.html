Keri Russell Net Worth

Keri Russell is an American actress and dancer with a net worth of $12 million.

Russell is perhaps best known for her role as Felicity in the WB drama “Felicity,” though she has had a number of other roles over the years. Her salary whilst filming Felicity was $30 Thousand per episode.

Keri Russell starred in the FX spy thriller series The Americans (2013–2018). Keri was nominated for an Emmy and several Golden Globe Awards after her role in “The Americans.”

During her time on “The Americans,” Keri appeared in films such as “Dark Skies,” “Austenland,” “Dawn of the Planet of the Apes,” and “Rise of the Planet of the Apes.” Both of the latter films were commercially successful.

She appeared in the 2016 film “Free State of Jones.” Following the conclusion of “The Americans,” Russell landed a role in “Star Wars: The Rise of Skywalker.”

Keri continues to appear in Broadway productions, most recently as Anna in “Burn This.” Russell is also a well-known voice actress who has played Wonder Woman in a number of animated projects. Keri received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2017.
