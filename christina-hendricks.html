Christina Hendricks News - Us Weekly

Name:Christina Hendricks

Hometown:Knoxville, TN

Birthday:May 3, 1975

About

Born on May 3, 1975, Christina Hendricks is best known for her role as Joan Harris on the television series Mad Men.

After a few small roles on television shows like ER, Firefly and Cold Case, the curvy redhead landed her breakout role on the AMC drama in 2007.

In October 2009, Hendricks wed fellow actor Geoffrey Arend. The twosome called it quits 10 years later.

She shares two Screen Actors Guild Awards with the cast of Mad Men for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble.

In March 2023, Hendricks announced her engagement to Good Girls camera operator George Bianchini.
