Reported Details On Sasha Banks Missing Royal Rumble Due To Injury

Sasha Banks‘ reasoning for missing an appearance at this year’s women’s Royal Rumble match is due to injury, several reports state.

Sean Ross Sapp of Fightful first gave out word that according to several sources, Sasha was absent from action because of an injury and Mike Johnson of PWInsider adds that the reported injury is reasoning for why her story with Lacey Evans has involved their scheduled matches getting cancelled. Johnson adds that Banks was planned to be in the Rumble up until Saturday, but the injury is what ultimately kept her from competing.  Although not confirmed by PWInsider, word is going around that the injury may be ankle-related.
