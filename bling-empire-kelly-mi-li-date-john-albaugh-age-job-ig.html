John Albaugh Job

John Albaugh is a Financial Management Specialist. He has over 11 years of experience in Accenture deals; especially with forecasting/budgeting, pricing/modeling, and general financial expertise.

In 2022, he was working as a “Client Financial Management Specialist” at Accenture providing ongoing business advisory and financial management services for client-facing outsourcing & consulting work

Also, John was then working at a company called Primates which he founded in 2017. Per their site, Primates was created to extend across various visual art platforms to bring ideas to life for businesses and individuals alike. The company claimed to churn out fantastic visuals, but more importantly, to deliver their’s client’s vision as it was imagined, on time, with no surprises.

“I’ve spent my whole life designing and illustrating. So, now, I’m passionate about bridging that elusive gap between form and function,” John explained why he created Primates.

So far, Primates has worked with Red Bull, Heifer International, Latino Film Institute, Wellteam Leadership, Paper Antler,  and many more.

Besides Primates, John is also the founder of Stables Comic. He started the webcomic in 2020.

Talking about John’s previous jobs, he interned at Defense Security Service for 4 months in 2010, worked with ARAMARK Refreshment Services for 10 months in 2009, was an associate at West Suburban Bank for 3 months in 2008, and a cashier at Sports Authority for 3 months in 2007.

Now, John’s in the limelight, he’s modeling career is taking off too. To this date, he has modeled for Good Circles Clothing, Kohl’s, and more.

The last we checked, he was signed to Wilhelmina Models.
