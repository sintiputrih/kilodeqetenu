Emily Ratajkowski Pairs a 90s Column Skirt With a Cropped Dress Shirt

Emily Ratajkowski is bringing back a ’90s silhouette: the low-rise column skirt.

Today, the model stepped out for a stroll in New York City wearing a silky long black skirt from Silk Laundry with a sleek white crop top that looked like a twisted dress shirt with billowing sleeves by House of CB. She finished the look with light brown leather pointed-toe boots with silver stud details, a golden chain belt, a slouchy black bag that also had a golden chain belt, and her go-to elongated black sunglasses. EmRata’s abs were entirely exposed in the unexpected look, and she wore her straight red hair down.

BACKGRID

Ratajkowski shared a selfie in the transitional ensemble on her Instagram Story, as well as a video of herself in the outfit on TikTok. The clip was set to Ian Asher’s viral “Nanana x Cola” edit.

The My Body author regularly shows off her unique sense of style while out in the city. Earlier this week, she was even spotted in a part of controversial bottoms: the jorts. EmRata was photographed walking through the city in a light-wash pair by EB Denim, which she styled with a white midriff-baring tube top from Cotton On. She finished off the look with blue-and-black sneakers by Vans, a black crescent moon shoulder bag from Loewe, black Lu Goldie x Taylor LaShae sunglasses, and layers of gold necklaces.

And late last month, she opted for a more feminine off-duty look composed of a skintight pink minidress with frilly red lace and a bold red rose print, from Guizio. She wore the mesh mini with white-and-pink Puma Palermo sneakers and elevated the look with a sleek brown crossbody bag by Loewe, oversize black square sunglasses, big gold hoops, and her gold Sylvester necklace.
