Seth Gold: Reality Star with a Net Worth of $1 Million

Seth Gold is a television reality star with a net worth of $1 million. He became famous as the star of the American documentary reality television series Hardcore Pawn. The show was about the daily activities of an American Jewelry and Loan family-owned pawn shop located in Detroit, Michigan. Seth Gold handles the marketing for this family owned and ran shop, owned by Les Gold, who is the son of the pawnbroker who originally owned Sam’s Loans. The assistant manager of American Jewelry and Loan is Seth’s sister Ashley, and each episode of Hardcore Pawn shows the sibling rivalry between Seth and Ashley.

Hardcore Pawn debuted on August 16, 2010, and set a new record with 2 million viewers as truTV’s most-watched series debut of all time. The series ended in 2015 after nine seasons and 162 seasons. Seth Gold was also an executive producer of Hardcore Pawn from 2011 to 2015.

In addition to Hardcore Pawn, Seth Gold has appeared on episodes of popular TV series such as Fox and Friends, Big Morning Buzz Live, The Arsenio Hall Show, Home & Family, The Playboy Morning Show, Today, and LeagueOne: In the Spotlight!.
