Brooke Shields’ Husband

Brooke Shields is married to Chris Henchy, a successful producer and screenwriter.

Henchy has worked on popular comedy films and TV shows like Entourage, Land of the Lost, The Campaign, and The Other Guys.

He co-created the comedy website Funny or Die with Will Ferrell and Adam McKay and was a partner in Gary Sanchez Productions.

Shields and Henchy have been together for over 20 years and have two daughters, Rowan and Grier.

According to Shields, laughter is the key to their enduring relationship, as Henchy’s sense of humor helps her stay grounded and not take things too seriously

He and Shields got engaged in 2000

Henchy proposed to Shields during their Mexican vacation in 2000.

Shields’ publicist confirmed the engagement, though details were kept private.

New York City jeweler Stephen Russell revealed information about Shields’ engagement ring.

The ring was described as an antique cushion-cut diamond set in platinum with a diamond mounting.
