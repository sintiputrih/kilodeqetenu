Meet Barcas History Maker Lamine Yamal; His Wiki and Family Life

Lamine Yamal is a Spanish professional football player who plays as a forward for the La Liga club, Barcelona. He has also played internationally for Spain in their U-15, U-16, U-17, and U-19 squads.

Recently, Lamine made history by becoming the youngest-ever player to feature for FC Barcelona in La Liga. On Sunday, 20 August 2023, Lamine became the youngest player to start in La Liga in the 21st century.

In Barcelona’s league game against Cadiz CF, Lamine became the history-maker at just 16 years and 38 days old. In his record-setting start, Barcelona triumphed over Cadiz with a convincing 2-0 victory.

Barcelona’s coach Xavi was happy to see the young kid make his full debut for the club. In the post-match interview, the legend himself said,

I think he’s so mature and can already make the difference for us. We have to take care of him, but I think he’s ready and I’m not afraid to trust youth.

After starting his youth career at Barcelona, Lamine was initially selected by Xavi to train with the first team in early September 2022. Eventually, he made his first-team debut on 29 April 2023 as an 83rd-minute substitute.

Lamine Yamal Wiki and Family: Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Height

Lamine Yamal Nasraoui Ebana was born in 2007 in Esplugues de Llobregat, Spain. He is 16 years of age and he celebrates his birthday on the 13th of July every year.

Talking about his family, Lamine was raised by his parents of mixed heritage in Barcelona. His father, Mounir Nasraoui is originally from Morocco. Likewise, his mother, Sheila Ebana comes from Equatorial Guinea.

Lamine’s father Mounir is originally from Morocco (Source: Mounir’s Instagram)

Both of Lamine’s parents often mention their son on their respective social networking sites. They have always been proud of their child and are enjoying how he is making history with one of the best teams in the world.

With Spanish nationality, Lamine has mixed ethnicity. Lamine stands tall at a height of 5 feet and 11 inches (1.8 meters).

How Much Is Lamine Yamal Salary and Net Worth?

Lamine commenced his youth career at Barcelona in 2014. He played for the Barcelona B side before joining the main rooster of FC Barcelona.

Lamine was called by FC Barcelona coach Xavi to train with the first team with other youngsters in September 2022. Although he was yet to sign his first professional contract with the team, he was able to impress the Barcelona manager.

Lamine became the youngest player ever to start in La Liga in the 21st century (Source: Lamine’s Instagram)

Eventually, Lamine made his first-team debut on 29 April 2023 replacing Gavi in the 83rd minute in a game against Real Betis. At that time, he became the youngest player to ever appear for Barcelona’s first team.

Lamine played for the Spain under-15s in 2021 where he scored 3 goals in 6 games. He then played four games and scored one goal for Spain under-16s in 2021.

Following that, Lamine scored 8 goals in 10 appearances for Spain under-17s in 2023. He now plays for the Spain under-19s.

As of now, Lamine’s salary and net worth remain undisclosed.
