Crystal Golf Solitaire - Play Online

Crystal Golf Solitaire game info

Crystal Golf Solitaire is a charming version of golf solitaire. As always your goal is to remove all the cards to reach the next round. The rules are similar to Fairway Solitaire.

Cards can be removed from the layout if they are one higher or lower than visible deck card shown at the bottom. You can set the difficulty level of the game before you beginning. On the easiest setting Aces are both high and low, allowing you to place either a King or 2 on them. On the two more difficult settings, Aces are low only, so you can only play a 2 on them. The most difficult setting also hides cards.

Jokers can also be used. These can be played at any time and any card can be placed on top of them and can be particularly useful in creating longer card runs.

At the end of each round you will receive bonus points for the number of unused cards and a speed bonus.

Click on the deck at the bottom to deal a new card. Crystal Golf Solitaire ends when no more moves are available.

System requirements

Crystal Golf Solitaire can be played in all modern browsers, on all device types (desktop, tablet, mobile), and on all operating systems (Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS, ...).
