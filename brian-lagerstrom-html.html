Brian Lagerstrom - Trivia, Family, Bio

Professional chef and baker known for cooking food at the professional level, then turning those techniques into approachable, easy-to-replicate dishes. He's also known for posting improved versions of classic dishes and bread-baking techniques. His self-titled YouTube channel has gained 1.2 million subscribers and 131 million views. 

Before Fame

He started cooking professionally in the year 2008 in the city of Chicago. 

Trivia

He has worked at restaurants as a line cook, a chef, and as a bread baker. His most viral YouTube video (with over 4 million views) is called "1 DOUGH 3 LOAVES | The Easiest (Actually Good) Bread You Can Make."

Family Life

He and his wife Lorn began posting cooking videos on YouTube in the year 2020. 

Associated With

In a December of 2021 YouTube video, he shared his thoughts about chef James Kenji López-Alt.
