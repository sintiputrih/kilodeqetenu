AEW Collision February 3, 2024 Preview: Confirmed Matches, Start Time and How to Watch

AEW remains committed to providing an entertaining experience for fans, and the upcoming February 3rd edition of Collision appears set to uphold this trend. The event promises numerous exciting elements for the audience to eagerly anticipate.

The February 3rd, 2024 edition of AEW Collision will emanate from the Edinburg, Texas’ Bert Ogden Arena and will be airing on TNT at 8 PM ET. Fans are certainly excited for the episode as well.

The upcoming show will showcase the latest trios match for FTR and Daniel Garcia, as they face off against The Patriarchy—Christian Cage, The Prodigy Nick Wayne, and Killswitch.

Fans can also anticipate an unexpected Bryan Danielson match against CMLL’s resident technical wrestling expert, Hechicero. Additionally, Continental Crown champion Eddie Kingston will go one-on-one with the Bounty Hunter Bryan Keith.

AEW Collision February 3rd Lineup:

  • Bryan Danielson vs. Hechicero
  • Eddie Kingston vs. Bryan Keith
  • FTR and Daniel Garcia vs. The Patriarchy
