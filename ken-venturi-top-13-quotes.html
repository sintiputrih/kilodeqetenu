Ken Venturi Top 13 Quotes

Ken Venturi was a well-known American professional golfer and golf broadcaster who has passed away. On May 15, 1931, he was born, and on May 17, 2013, he died. During his PGA Tour career, he won 14 events, including a major, the US Open, in 1964. He was inducted into the World Golf Hall of Fame shortly before his death.

Venturi was born in San Francisco, California, and has been involved in the golf scene since he was a toddler. Following that, he joined Harding Park Golf Course and established himself as a professional golfer and one of the best golfers in the world. His entrance to Lincoln High School makes him the 1948 and 1949 San Francisco high school golf champion.
