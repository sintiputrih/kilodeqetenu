Becky Lynch To Appear On Celebrity Jeopardy On November 15

“The Man” is coming to Celebrity Jeopardy, as Becky Lynch will appear on the show.

WWE star Becky Lynch has been announced for the November 15 episode of Celebrity Jeopardy. She will compete against Home Alone star Macaulay Culkin and Rachel Dratch on the show. The winner will advance to the semifinals. The contests compete for a charity of their choosing.

Lynch is looking to bounce back after she lost the NXT Women’s Championship to Lyra Valkyria on the October 24 episode of WWE NXT. Xia Li confronted Lynch on the October 30 episode of WWE RAW and set up a match between the two.
