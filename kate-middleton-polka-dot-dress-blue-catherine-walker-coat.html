Kate Middleton Steps Out in Sandringham with Prince William &amp; Queen Elizabeth

  • Following a busy Christmas break, Kate Middleton and Prince William attended church on Sunday with Queen Elizabeth.
  • Stepping out on the Sandringham estate, the Duke and Duchess of Cambridge were greeted by well-wishers, and Kate was given a bunch of flowers.
  • The Duchess rewore one of her previous looks—a blue Catherine Walker coat which she debuted on her trip to Norway at the start of 2018, per the Daily Mail. Kate is well known for recycling her favorite looks, with Catherine Walker being one of her go-to brands.

The holiday season might be over, but several members of the royal family are still spending time in Sandringham.

Stepping out this morning for church, Kate Middleton looked chic in a blue Catherine Walker coat, which she wore with a matching headband. The Duchess of Cambridge was gifted with a beautiful bunch of flowers by a group of royal fans after the service.
