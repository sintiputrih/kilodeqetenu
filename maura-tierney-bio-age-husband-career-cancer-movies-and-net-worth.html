Maura Tierney Biography

Maura Tierney is an American actress famous for her roles as Lisa Miller on medical drama ER and Helen Solloway television drama The Affair for which she won a Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress – Series, Miniseries or Television Film in 2016.

Maura Tierney Early Life and Education

Being the eldest in a family of three, Tierney was born and brought up with her siblings in the Hyde Park neighborhood of Boston, Massachusetts, to a Catholic family. Her mother, Pat is a real estate broker and her late father, Joseph Tierney was a famous politician in Boston.

Tierney went to Mount Alvernia Academy Elementary School and Notre Dame Academy in Hingham, Massachusetts and studied drama. After her graduation, she went to New York University for further studies in drama and dance.
