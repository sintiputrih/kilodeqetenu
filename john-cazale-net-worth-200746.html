About

Remembered for his roles in such celebrated films as The Godfather, The Conversation, Dog Day Afternoon and The Deer Hunter, this acclaimed actor was known for his multi-layered portrayals of violent and unstable, yet vulnerable, characters.

Before Fame

He met his Future artistic collaborator, Al Pacino, while working for the New York City office of Standard Oil.

Trivia

Family Life

Born in Revere, Massachusetts, he grew up in a family of Italian heritage.

Associated With
