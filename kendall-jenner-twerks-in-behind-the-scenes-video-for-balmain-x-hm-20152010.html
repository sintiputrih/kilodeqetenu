Kendall Jenner Twerks in Behind-the-Scenes Video for Balmain x H&amp;M

Twerking what Kris Jenner gave her! The Balmain x H&M collection won’t debut until Thursday, Nov. 5, but the mass retailer just dropped this behind-the-scenes video of Kendall Jenner dancing up a storm as the star of the capsule’s video campaign.

In the teaser, released via Yahoo! Style on Oct. 19, Jenner, 19, can be seen rocking the label’s embellished styles with statement earrings and a Star Trek-worthy ponytail, all while learning how to break it down inside a tricked-out subway car from the future.

The budding supermodel took to Instagram on Oct. 20, to share her own sneak peek from the campaign: a close-up shot of her booty in a bedazzled tunic, which she cheekily captioned, “flashback to shooting #HMBalmaination. practicing a little twerk.”

That same day, Jenner and her sister, Kylie Jenner, 18, landed in NYC, where presumably they will be attending the collection’s epic launch party in the evening. While Kendall modeled a distressed tee with a nude jacket and dark-wash jeans, her younger sister, who held her hand through the airport, sported an oversized jade jacket with an olive dress and calf-high boots.

The sisters are back to work now that their estranged brother-in-law Lamar Odom is back in L.A. and on the mend after being found unconscious in Las Vegas on Tuesday, Oct. 13.
