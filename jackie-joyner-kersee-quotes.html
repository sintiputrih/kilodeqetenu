52 famous Jackie Joyner-Kersee Quotes

Jackie Joyner-Kersee is an American former track and field athlete who was ranked among the all-time greatest athletes in the heptathlon and long jump. She won three gold, two bronze Olympic medals and one silver in those two events at four separate Olympic Games. She was also voted for the Greatest Female Athlete of All-Time by Sports Illustrated for Women magazine.

Moreover, she contributes through the board of Directors for USA Track and Field (USATF), which is the national governing body of the sport. Currently, she is an active philanthropist in children’s education, women’s right and racial equality. She was recognized broadly after founding the Jackie Joyner-Kersee Foundation that inspires most of the youngsters in East St. Louis towards athletes and academics.

Similarly, she has been participating actively in technology as she partnered with Comcast to establish the Internet Essentials program in 2011. Apart from her career and profession, she has her quotes that are given below. You can choose your favorite one.
