Circle The Drain

Check out Circle The Drain (Prod By Tricky Stewart), the latest track from Katy Perry, which dropped on Tuesday, August 10th, 2010. Katy Perry's movement grows stronger with each new release, and Circle The Drain (Prod By Tricky Stewart) has further strengthened that fact. It's a solid addition to the commendable body of work Katy Perry has been creating since stepping foot in the rap game, and we're definitely looking forward to what Katy Perry has on deck.

Hear more of Katy Perry on Firework (Prod. by Stargate). If you're down, make sure to visit Katy Perry's profile page for the newest info, news, songs, and mixtapes. Follow Katy Perry on Facebook and Twitter, if you aren't already.
