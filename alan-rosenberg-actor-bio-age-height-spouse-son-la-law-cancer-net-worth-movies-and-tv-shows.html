Alan Rosenberg Biography

Alan Rosenberg is an American award-winning Actor and Director who was born and brought-up in Passaic, New Jersey. He is widely known for his role as Eli Levinson in the NBC legal drama television series ‘L.A. Law’ and in the ABC legal drama series ‘Civil Wars’. He has worked the president of the Screen Actors Guild, the 27th principal motion picture industry on-screen performers’ union. He has appeared on notable films and series including ‘ER’ as Samuel Gasner, earning an Emmy Award nomination, ‘Shameless’ as Professor Youens and in ‘Barnaby Jones’ as Ray Reed.
