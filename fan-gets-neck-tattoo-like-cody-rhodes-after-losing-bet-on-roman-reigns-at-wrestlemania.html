Fan Gets Neck Tattoo Like Cody Rhodes After Losing Bet on Roman Reigns at WrestleMania

Cody Rhodes was immediately presented as a huge deal upon his return to WWE at WrestleMania 39. Things only got better for him after his return at the Royal Rumble, following a long absence due to injury. Rhodes then faced Roman Reigns at WrestleMania 39 but wasn’t able to beat him. Now it seems a fan got Rhodes’ neck tattoo after betting that Reigns would lose at WrestleMania 39.

All signs pointed towards The American Nightmare finishing the story and toppling The Tribal Chief at WrestleMania 39. For numerous instances during the match, fans believed that was exactly what would happen.

Unfortunately, that was not the case as Roman Reigns successfully retained the title, following interference from The Bloodline. This move was seen as a huge blunder by many people.

As revealed by a Reddit user named SarcasticFlamez, a friend of his had bet that Roman Reigns would lose at the Show of Shows. After losing the bet, the fan ended up getting Cody Rhodes’ neck tattoo on his own neck.

Cody Rhodes was also decimated by Brock Lesnar on this past week’s episode of Monday Night RAW. Fans continue to feel the sting of Cody Rhodes losing to Roman Reigns and it is unlikely to subside anytime soon.
