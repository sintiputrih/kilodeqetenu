Catriona Gray Height, Age, Boyfriend, Biography, Net worth, Family Fact

Catriona Gray (born January 6, 1994) is a Filipino-Australian Model, Television Host, Singer, and Beauty Pageant Titleholder from Cairns, Queensland. She is famous for being the Miss Universe Philippines 2018 and Miss World Philippines 2016. Furthermore, the 24-year-old model first crowned Miss World Philippines 2016 on October 2, 2016.

Later, she participated in the Miss World 2016 and took place in the Top 5. However, Stephanie Del Valle took the crown. Later, on 18 March 2018, Gray won the Miss Universe Philippines 2018 title.

Currently, she competing in the 67th Miss Universe pageant. the event will be held on December 17, 2018, at Impact, Muang Thong Thani in Bangkok, Thailand. Francesca Hung, Andrea Toscano, Nehal Chudasama, Helena Heuser, Mayra Dias etc are some of her competitors.

On December 13, 2018, Gray walked onstage during the preliminary competition. Moreover, she decided to wear the Ibong Adarna-inspired gown which stunned the judges and her fans.

Her gown was designed by Mak Tumang. Mak also said that he also took inspiration from the Phoenix other than Ibong Adarna. The 24-year-old beauty queen is also extremely popular on Instagram because of her talent and looks. Her real full birth name is Maria Catriona Elisa Magnayon Gray.

Profile, Parents & Early Life

This model was born on the 6th of January 1994 in Cairns, Queensland, Australia. Therefore, Catriona Gray’s age is 24 years old, as of 2018. Her father “Ian Gray” is from Fraserburgh, and her mother “Normita Ragas Magnayon” from Oas, Albay, Philippines. Hence, the beauty queen belongs to mixed ethnicity.

Furthermore, she attended Trinity Anglican School for her high school education. Later, Maria earned her Certificate in Music Theory from the Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts.

In addition, the model also received a Certificate in Outdoor Recreation. As of relationship, Gray is dating her boyfriend Clint Bondad (Filipino-German model). The couple started dating each other in 2013.
