Maury Povich Biography, Age, Height, Wife, Net Worth, Family

Age, Biography and Wiki

Maury Povich is an American television personality, best known for hosting the tabloid talk show Maury since 1991. He is also a former news anchor and talk show host. Povich was born in Washington, D.C., the son of Ruth (née Reimer) and Shirley Povich, a sportswriter for The Washington Post. He attended the University of Pennsylvania, where he was a member of the Pi Lambda Phi fraternity. Povich began his career in broadcasting in 1961, working as a news reporter for WTTG-TV in Washington, D.C. He later worked as a news anchor for WRC-TV in Washington, D.C., and then for WNAC-TV in Boston, Massachusetts. In 1991, Povich began hosting the tabloid talk show Maury, which has since become one of the longest-running talk shows in television history. He has also hosted the game show Twenty-One and the syndicated version of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire. As of 2021, Maury Povich's net worth is estimated to be $60 million.
