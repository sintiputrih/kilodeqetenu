Get To Know Annemarie Wiley Kids: Morocca, Ariya, Alivia, Marcus

Annemarie Wiley, the star of Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills (RHOBH) is a mother to four kids namely Morocca, Ariya, Alivia, and Marcus Wiley Jr. Learn about their age, and current residence, along with other additional details.

Tag along and learn more as this article proceeds.

Who Are RHOBH Annemarie Wiley Kids?

Annemarie Wiley, a mother to four children and the star of Real House Of Beverly Hills for the 13th season, welcomed three of her kids with her former NFL defensive end husband Marcellus Wiley. But since the network hasn’t yet made any official casting announcements, it’s unknown if the anesthesiologist and mother of four will be a diamond holder or a supporting co-star (also known as a “friend of”).

Annemarie and Marcellus share three children—son Marcellus Jr., and daughters Ariya Jayne, and Alivia Marie—as well as Marcellus Sr.’s daughter Morocca Alise from a previous relationship. The couple has been married to each other since 2014 and they are together starting from 2012.

Annemarie is a Nurse Anesthetist and co-founder of Project Transition. She is currently 41 years old and was born in April 1982. Her maiden name is Krygsveld.

A former American football player with a $5 million net worth, Marcellus is currently a TV personality. As an NFL defensive end for the Buffalo Bills, San Diego Chargers, Dallas Cowboys, and Jacksonville Jaguars, Marcellus Wiley’s net worth increased. He gave up playing in 2006. Wiley, a native of California, began his undergraduate career at Columbia University, where he played defensive end, tailback, and for the Lions.

The Buffalo Bills were impressed with his play and took him in the 1997 NFL Draft. Soon after, the 6 foot 4 player began his professional career, mostly as a pass rusher. After Bruce Smith departed the team in 2000 to join the Washington Redskins, the player transitioned to the position of defensive end.

Marcellus signed on with the Chargers in 2001, when he was chosen for the AFC Pro Bowl squad. He spent three seasons with the Chargers. Before joining his fourth and last team, the Washington Redskins, Marcellus played for the Dallas Cowboys for one season. In 2006, he played his last game. He has served as an NFL commentator on ESPN since his retirement. Also, he and Max Kellerman co-host an ESPN discussion sports program. He is a co-founder of Prolebrity, a sports community where athletes advertise their organizations, causes, and events.

Meet Morocca Wiley, Annemarie Wiley Step-Daughter

Morocca Wiley is the step-daughter of Annemarie Wiley born to Marcellus Wiley from his previous relationship to Kim Pointdexter. She is currently 24 years old.

Morocca hailed from Charlottesville, Virginia. She is living in Glastonbury, Connecticut. She attended Glastonbury High School and completed her college education at UConn. In high school, she was a track-and-field athlete. She earned nine varsity letters in cross country and track at Glastonbury High School. She also played one year of varsity soccer where her team won the state championship and was on the honor roll all four years of high school.

In college, Morocca competed in all indoor, outdoor, and cross-country events. She stands tall at 5 feet 9 inches.

Find her on FB and IG.

As for her mother, Kim is currently married to her husband Anthony Pointdexter. Kim and Anthony have been married since 25 February 2001.

Meet Ariya Wiley, Annemarie Wiley Younger Daughter

Annemarie Wiley’s younger daughter is named Ariya Wiley. Ariya turned 4 years old in January 2023.

On her 4th birthday, Annemarie wished her daughter, “Happy 4th birthday to our beautiful, silly, captivating, strong-willed, Encanto and Moana singing, gymnastics loving, non-stop giggling, all over the house dancing, guitar playing rock star!🤩We love you beyond words, Ariya Jayne.”

Ariya has her own IG account (@ariyajwiley).

Meet Alivia Wiley, Annemarie Wiley Youngest Daughter

Alivia Wiley is the youngest of three children Annemarie Wiley shares with her husband. On 26 March 2023, Alivia celebrated her 3rd birthday.

Annemarie announced her daughter’s birth via an IG post where she wrote, “Our “Itty Bitty” with the larger than life personality 🎉I am so proud of my ever giggling and singing, silly, fun-loving little Liv Liv! …You are mommy’s little ray of sunshine! 🥰I love you beyond words, best friend! 💖 Happy 3️⃣rd Birthday, my Alivia Marie.”

Back in 2020, Annemarie and Marcellus shared the news of welcoming their daughter via an IG post. She captioned the post, “The boys are officially outnumbered! Please welcome Alivia Marie Wiley 💖 Born 3/26 @ 11:26 pm. A week early to join the party! 7lbs 14 oz, 21.5 inches long. She was, by far, mommy’s easiest and fastest labor – 3rd time truly is a charm!🙌🏾🙏🏾 She is an Aries♈️ like her mommy though… so maybe I shouldn’t hold my breath 😆 Thank you everyone for all the love and well wishes! We are doing fantastic and enjoying the calm before we go home to the storm👦🏽👧🏽👶🏽 …#2under2 #3under5 #Wileypartyof6.”

Meet Marcellus Wiley Jr, Annemarie Wiley Son

Annemarie Wiley has a son named Marcellus ‘MJ’ Wiley Jr. Marcellus is 7 years old. On his 7th birthday in August 2022, Annemarie stated that she loves being a mom because of him. She described her son as “Brilliant, kind, talented, loving, hilarious, determined, curious, empathetic- you simply amaze me, my Ninjago-loving little genius.”
