Who is Arielle Vandenberg?

Arielle Vandenberg is an actress and model from America. She rose to fame from her role in the 2007 film Epic Movie.

Furthermore, she has also starred in hit TV shows such as How I Met Your Mother and CSI: Miami.

Arielle Vandenberg was born on September 27, 1986, in Los Angeles, California, United States.

She is the daughter of  Dirk Vandenberg and DeEtte Vandenberg.

She grew up in Fallbrook, California along with her parents. As a child, she had a keen interest in dancing and also began to learn at the age of five.

Talking about her nationality, Arielle Vandenberg is American and her ethnicity is mixed (Dutch, 1/8th Italian, English, Irish, German, Welsh, French).
