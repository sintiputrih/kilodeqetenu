Is Vanessa Kirby Married? Who Is Her Partner? Her Dating History

Vanessa Kirby might star as a villain in Mission: Impossible 7, but she’s been a hero to many fans, and even more to her partners.

So, is the actor married now? If not, who’s her partner? Keep reading to find the answer.

Is Vanessa Kirby Married?

No, Vanessa Kirby isn’t married. However, she plans to get married soon and start a family of her own one day. But for the time being, she’s happy advising her friends on pregnancy and parenthood.

“I am so obsessed with everyone’s birthing stories after doing Pieces of a Woman,” Vanessa shared. “With my best friend, Anna, when she told me she was pregnant, I would be like, ‘So basically you are on month four, so now…’ Who am I to start giving people advice about it? I have never bloody done it!”

Who Is Vanessa Kirby Partner?

Vanessa Kirby is in a relationship with her partner Paul Rabil. The two were last seen as they departed Chiltern Firehouse in London in March 2023. For the occasion, Vanessa bundled up in a long wool coat with a pair of black leather trousers layered underneath while Paul wore a light grey jumper with a pair of smart trousers and some white trainers.

Sadly, we don’t know when the duo exactly started dating as they never featured each other on their social media. However, they’ve been a pair since September 2022. At the time, the lovebirds were first seen leaving the Crosby Hotel together and the next month, they were spotted strolling around in NYC.

Prior to Vanessa, her partner was in a relationship with the Mexican actress Eiza Gonzalez and was married to fellow sportswoman Kelly Berger, from 2014 until 2017. 

For those who don’t know, Paul Rabil is the co-founder of the Premiere Lacrosse League. Also, he’s a partner of Rabil Ventures and co-founder of Rabil Companies. But before all this, Paul used to be a Lacrosse player himself who played for the US for over 12 years. So far, he’s played college lacrosse at Johns Hopkins University where he was named a 4x All-American. Furthermore, Rabil is a 2x World Champion, a 3x Professional Lacrosse Champion, a 3x Professional Lacrosse MVP, and a 2x NCAA Division I Champion.

In 2019, Rabil was also selected for the PLL All-Star Game.

Vanessa Kirby Dating History

Prior to Paul, Vanessa Kirby was linked to Christopher Abbott for a while. The pair first met when they played a married couple in 19th Century romantic drama The World To Come in 2020.

And before Paul, the Mission: Impossible star dated Callum Turner from 2015 to 2019. The two first met as co-stars in 2014’s Queen and Country. Over the next four 4 years, the two then “gradually [grew] apart” in a “very amicable” way.

A source said, “The split happened a few weeks ago and it wasn’t ugly at all. Their work schedules just weren’t aligning and it put a strain on their relationship. They’re going to remain friends though!”

During the time Vanessa was with Callum, she was also rumored to have linked with Tom Cruise after a photo of the two kissing — while in character and on set — somehow made the rounds as proof of a relationship. “The most disconcerting thing was the people I didn’t think would believe it, did, and they were texting my boyfriend, asking, ‘Are you OK?’ I don’t think they believe anything they read now because you just can’t. None of it is real. To be getting married to him? Not even a snog, it was marriage,”  Vanessa later slammed the rumor.

  • Who Are Vanessa Kirby Parents?

Vanessa was born to her parents, Roger and Jane Kirby who each come from humble beginnings.

The actor’s mother, Jane lost her parents at a young age. She then ended up in Paris by herself, with only enough money for one baked potato a day before she met Roger. “She would mix water and Marmite and that was the sauce. So, they had this incredible thing where they were like, you can do anything,” Vanessa shared.

But though Vanessa has such strong parents, she had so much self-doubt growing up. “Oh my goodness, I still do. I have made peace with the fact that it is there,” she explained. “It’s the practice of letting it be in the passenger seat. You have to say: ‘You are not going to drive but you can be here’.”

Fortunately, Vanessa has Christopher McQuarrie who lent her loads of books on power and how to transmit power — “the more you reel it in, the more intimidating it is,” the actor shared.

Besides her parents, Vanessa was close to her siblings Juliet and Joe Kirby.
