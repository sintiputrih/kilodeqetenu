Meghan Markle &amp; Prince Harry Turn Off Comments on Their Instagram Account

It's the end of an era for Duchess Meghan and Prince Harry. Following news that the couple has reportedly moved to Los Angeles, the Sussexes have now switched off the comments on their Instagram account.

As reported by Town & Country, "Previously posted comments are no longer visible, and users are unable to add new ones." The change comes as Meghan and Harry step down from their roles as senior members of the royal family. Fans are likely to be upset about the change, particularly as the Sussexes amassed such a large following in a short space of time.

As part of their agreement with the royal family, Meghan and Harry won't use the word "royal" in any future work endeavors. Per the couple's website, "While The Duke and Duchess are focused on plans to establish a new non-profit organization, given the specific UK government rules surrounding use of the word 'Royal,' it has been therefore agreed that their non-profit organization will not utilize the name 'Sussex Royal' or any other iteration of 'Royal.'"

A source recently told People, "They are positive about the future... It is exciting for them to mark the end of the transition and the official start of their new chapter."
