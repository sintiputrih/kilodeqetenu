On 18-12-1924 Cicely Tyson was born in New York City, New York. She made her 13.5 million dollar fortune with How to Get Away with Murder, Sweet Justice & The Help. The actress is currently single, her starsign is Sagittarius and she is now 98 years of age.

Cicely Tyson is an actress and a former fashion model. She became known for portraying strong African-American women both and the screen, as well as on the stage throughout her long career. She is the recipient of three Primetime Emmy Awards, four Black Reel Awards, one Screen Actor Guild Award and one Tony Award. In November 2016, she was awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom – the highest civilian honor in the United States.

In 1981, she married jazz trumpeter, Miles Davis. Unfortunately, the marriage did not last and the two got divorced in 1988.
Cicely Tyson has no children.
