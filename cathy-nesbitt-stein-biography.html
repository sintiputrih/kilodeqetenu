Who is Cathy Nesbitt-Stein?

Cathy Nesbitt-Stein is an American reality star and a dancer. She is well-known as the mother of the extremely popular dancer, Vivi-Anne Stein. Vivi is a Dance Moms Competition star.

Although her daughter has played a major role in her rise as a reality star, Cathy’s talent cannot be ignored. Likewise, she is the owner of the popular dance studio, Candy Apple’s Dance Center.

As a reality Tv Show star, Cathy Nesbitt-Stein appeared for seasons one, two, and three of the reality Tv Show, Dance Moms. She has been a talented entertainer and dancer since her childhood. Moreover, she was the first person cast for the show.
