Alex Aiono Biography and Wiki

Alex Aiono is an acclaimed American Singer, Youtuber, Producer and Actor who was born and brought up in Phoenix, Arizona, United States. He is widely known for his Youtube videos which has amassed over 6 million followers and over 191 million viewers.

He released his debut single ‘Work the Middle’ through Interscope Records in February 2017. In April the same year, he released his second single titled ‘Question.

He released his third single ‘Does It Feel Like Falling’ on 18th September the same year 2017. He is featured in an upcoming Netflix film called Finding Ohana as a starring character.
