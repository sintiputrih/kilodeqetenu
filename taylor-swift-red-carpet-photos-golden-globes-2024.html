Taylor Swift Shines in an Electric-Green Sequin Gown at the 2024 Golden Globes

Taylor Swift doesn’t break records only in music—now, she’s also topping the film charts. This year, the superstar is nominated for a Golden Globe for her Eras Tour movie.

The “Lover” singer showed up at the award show tonight in a shimmering sequin gown in an electric green. The custom Gucci piece designed by Sabato De Sarno featured a cupped bra, thin shoulder straps, and an open back embellished with three thin straps.

Swift accessorized the bold, Reputation-esque piece with strings of diamond jewelry by De Beers, including various stacked, cascading earrings, a large ear cuff, a dangly bracelet, and several rings.

Her glittery silver manicure matched her sparkly look. The singer wore minimal makeup—including a subtle black liner and a glossy pink lip—and had her bronde hair styled in pretty, casual waves, with blown-out bangs.

Monica Schipper/GA//Getty Images

Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour is up for an award in one of the Golden Globes’ two new categories, Cinematic and Box Office Achievement. It is up against this year’s biggest films, Barbie and Oppenheimer, which are leading the show’s nominations list with nine and eight nods, respectively. Other contenders in the category are Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3, John Wick: Chapter 4, Mission: Impossible – Dead Reckoning Part 1, Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse, and The Super Mario Bros. Movie.

Swift’s three-house concert film includes performances of songs from all 10 of her studio albums. It premiered this October and immediately broke records for first-day ticket sales, thanks to the singer’s army of Swifties. It has since grossed more than $200 million at the global box office and taken its place as the biggest concert-film debut of all time. The movie became available for streaming (on Apple TV, Prime Video, YouTube, Xfinity, Google Play, and Vudu) on Swift’s 34th birthday, December 13.
