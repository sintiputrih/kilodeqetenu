Who is Jason Kelce Married to?

Jason Kelce, the renowned "King of Philly" due to his exceptional passion for the city and his stellar football career with the Philadelphia Eagles, is married to Kylie Kelce (née McDevitt). Their marriage has been a subject of interest, especially after Jason reportedly expressed his intention to retire from professional football.

The Marriage of Jason and Kylie Kelce

Jason and Kylie Kelce tied the knot on April 14, 2018, at the Logan Hotel in Philadelphia. The wedding was a joyous occasion attended by approximately 170 guests, including family and friends. Notably, the couple's 20-pound Irish wolfhound, Winnie, was also present at the ceremony, highlighting the significance of their beloved pet in their lives.

The couple's wedding was a particularly special event as it followed the Philadelphia Eagles' victory in Super Bowl LII just a couple of months earlier. The wedding marked the beginning of a new chapter in their lives and garnered attention due to their status as public figures in the city.

Relationship Origins

Kylie and Jason's relationship journey traces back to their meeting on the dating app Tinder. Their relationship became public in 2015 when Kylie posted a black-and-white picture of the two on her Instagram account, expressing gratitude that Jason had also swiped right. Their relationship then blossomed, culminating in their marriage in 2018.

Family Life

The Kelce family has expanded since their marriage, and they now have three daughters together: Wyatt Elizabeth, Elliotte Ray, and Bennett Llewellyn. Their youngest daughter was welcomed into the world in February 2023, shortly after Super Bowl LVII, further cementing the joyous moments in their family life.

Reflections on Marriage

The couple's wedding was a memorable event, with Jason's perseverance and approach to life deeply resonating with their wedding photographer, Sarah Alderman. Her emotional response to Jason's speech at the wedding highlighted the impact of his mentality and life philosophy, further emphasizing the significance of their union.

Jason Kelce is married to Kylie Kelce (née McDevitt), and their marriage has been an integral part of their personal and public lives. Their journey from the early days of their relationship to their wedding and the subsequent expansion of their family has been a source of interest and inspiration for many. As Jason considers retirement from professional football, their enduring partnership stands as a testament to their shared experiences and unwavering commitment to each other.
